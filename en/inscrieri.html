Frequently Asked Questions

Any student of "Mircea cel Bătrân" National College, regardless of profile or year of study. For external events (county-level conferences), we may also accept delegates from other schools — we'll announce when applicable.

Basic membership is free. External conference participation may involve registration fees (covered by each delegate). For conferences we organize ourselves, the fee will be communicated in the official announcement.

Your application is evaluated by the Executive Team. Within 7 days you'll receive an email with the result. If selected, you'll be invited to a first onboarding meeting where we present the club structure and upcoming activities.

Regular club activities take 3-5 hours per week on average (workshops, training sessions, meetings). During conference periods (intensive prep or participation), time can grow to 8-12 hours weekly for a few weeks.

For internal activities and local conferences, Romanian is sufficient. For international conferences (or to read official UN documents), you need intermediate-to-advanced English. The club also offers English public speaking workshops for those who want to prepare.
