Welcome to the official MUN: CNMB website

Announcements May 16, 2026 · 2 min read · by MUN: CNMB Team

We are thrilled to announce the official launch of the MUN: CNMB website — Model United Nations: "Mircea cel Bătrân" National College — the first club of its kind in the entire Vâlcea county.

Starting today, you'll find here everything about club life: who we are, what we do, our team, the latest news, official documents, and application forms for our activities.

What you'll find on the website

  • About Us — the club's story, our mission, values, and objectives.
  • Our Team — all 23 leadership positions, from Coordinator and Executive Board to departments.
  • News — announcements, conferences, achievements, and behind-the-scenes content from the Department of Press.
  • Documents — Constitution, Rules of Procedure, and other important club documents.
  • Apply — forms for all our open activities.

How you can get involved

If you're a student at CNMB and interested in diplomacy, debate, or public speaking — apply to join the club. If you're a teacher, parent, or partner who wants to support us — write to us at contact@muncnmb.com.

"Lead the way to Tomorrow!" — this isn't just a motto, but a promise we make to ourselves: to shape tomorrow's leaders through the practice of today's diplomacy.

Thanks to the Department of IT for building this website and to the Executive Team for the vision that made everything possible. This is only the beginning — we invite you to be part of what comes next.

— The MUN: CNMB Team
