About Us

MUN: CNMB is an extracurricular club themed around Model United Nations, operating within "Mircea cel Bătrân" National College in Râmnicu Vâlcea. The club is led by students, coordinated by teachers, and organizes MUN conferences, workshops, and UN-themed activities. We are the first club of this kind in the entire Vâlcea county.

What we do

Three directions that shape the MUN: CNMB experience.

Training & Workshops

Preparation for delegates: country research, resolution writing, public speaking, professional writing.

Conferences

Participation in national and international MUN conferences plus organizing our own conference accessible to all of Vâlcea county.

Community

Team, leadership through frequent elections, diplomacy, networking, a place where students grow together.
