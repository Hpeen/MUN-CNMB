About Us

Who we are and what we do

Who we are

MUN: CNMB: Model United Nations: "Mircea cel Bătrân" National College - is an extracurricular club themed around Model United Nations, operating within "Mircea cel Bătrân" National College in Râmnicu Vâlcea. The club is led by students, coordinated by teachers, and organizes MUN conferences, workshops, and UN-themed activities. We are the first club of this kind in the entire Vâlcea county.

Founders: Andrei D. Tulpan (President), Matei H. Găman (Vice President), and Valeria C. Antonescu (General Coordinator).

Our motto is "Lead the Way to Tomorrow!", because the diplomacy, debate, and leadership learned today will shape the leaders of tomorrow.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To offer the students of "Mircea cel Bătrân" National College and the entire Vâlcea county an authentic platform to learn diplomacy, public speaking, professional writing, and critical thinking through simulating United Nations procedures.

Our Vision

To organize the first county-level MUN conference in Vâlcea and to shape a new generation of students ready to participate in national and international conferences.

Our Values

Three principles that guide every activity of the club.

Professionalism

Activities are conducted to a high standard, from country research to chairing committees.

Leadership

Frequent elections and limited mandates ensure continuous development across member generations.

Diplomacy

Diplomatic communication, active listening, and authentic representation of simulated countries.

Club objectives

  1. Preparing and sending students to national and international MUN conferences.
  2. Organizing an MUN conference accessible to the entire Vâlcea county.
  3. Developing members' research, writing, and public speaking skills.

What we organize

  • Model UN Conferences
  • MUN Workshops
  • External Conference Preparation
  • Other UN-themed activities
