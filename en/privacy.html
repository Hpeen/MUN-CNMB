Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

1. Who we are

MUN: CNMB (Model United Nations: "Mircea cel Bătrân" National College) is an extracurricular club based at "Mircea cel Bătrân" National College, Râmnicu Vâlcea, Vâlcea County, Romania. This policy explains what personal data we collect through the website www.muncnmbvl.com and how we use it.

2. What data we collect

Through the forms on this website, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Class and school profile
  • Free text fields you fill in (motivation, MUN experience, comments)
  • Languages spoken and weekly availability

We also automatically collect limited technical data:

  • Browser type, operating system, and device
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Pages visited and time spent

3. Why we collect this data

  • Processing membership applications and event registrations.
  • Communicating with you by email regarding your application or participation.
  • Improving the website through aggregated usage data analysis.
  • Legal compliance when necessary.

4. Legal basis (GDPR)

We process your data based on your explicit consent, given by checking the "I agree to data processing" box when submitting the form. You can withdraw consent at any time by contacting us at muncnmb.contact@gmail.com.

5. Who we share data with

Your data is accessible only to MUN: CNMB Executive Team members who process applications (primarily President and Vice-President) and to the teacher coordinator. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.

We use service providers (e.g., GitHub Pages for hosting, possibly Formspree for form processing) that process your data on our behalf, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. How long we keep data

  • Rejected applications: 6 months (in case of reapplication).
  • Accepted applications: duration of membership + 1 year after leaving the club.
  • Anonymous analytics: 24 months.

7. Minors' data

Our website is primarily addressed to high school students. For minors under 16, we require confirmation of parental/legal guardian consent when submitting the form. We recommend that parents discuss with their children before they submit personal data online.

8. Cookies

We use a minimal number of cookies, only for essential functionality:

  • cookiesAccepted, remembers acceptance of the cookie banner.
  • announcementDismissed, remembers that you closed the announcement bar.

We do not use advertising tracking cookies. If we activate Google Analytics in the future, we will add explicit consent for analytics cookies.

9. Your rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right:

  • To be informed about what data we collect.
  • To access your personal data.
  • To request rectification of incorrect data.
  • To request data deletion ("right to be forgotten").
  • To restrict processing.
  • To object to processing.
  • To request data portability.
  • To lodge a complaint with ANSPDCP (Romanian DPA).

To exercise any right, write to us at muncnmb.contact@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

10. Data security

The website uses HTTPS encryption for all data transmissions. Applications received by email are accessible only to authorized Executive Team accounts.

11. Policy changes

We reserve the right to update this policy. The current version is always available at www.muncnmbvl.com/en/privacy.html. The date of the last update appears at the top of this page.

12. Contact

For any questions about this policy or your personal data:
Email: muncnmb.contact@gmail.com
